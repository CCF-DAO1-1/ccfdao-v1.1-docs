Welcome to the **CKB Community Fund DAO 1.1** documentation! 

## What is CKB Community Fund DAO?

The **CKB Community Fund DAO** is a decentralized autonomous organization designed to support the Nervos CKB ecosystem through community-driven funding and governance.

### Key Features

- **Decentralized Governance**: Community-driven decision making
- **Transparent Funding**: All transactions are on-chain and verifiable
- **Open Participation**: Anyone can propose and vote on initiatives
