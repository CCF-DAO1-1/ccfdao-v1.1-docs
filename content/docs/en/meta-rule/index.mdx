## DAO v1.1 Governance Framework

The Meta Rule defines the governance framework, processes, and rules that govern the CKB Community Fund DAO v1.1. Understanding these rules is essential for effective participation in the DAO.

### What are Meta Rules?

Meta Rules are the foundational governance rules that define:

- How proposals are created, reviewed, and approved
- Voting mechanisms and quorum requirements
- Fund management and allocation processes
- How the rules themselves can be amended

## Why Meta Rules Matter

Meta Rules ensure:

- **Transparency**: All governance processes are clearly defined
- **Fairness**: Equal opportunity for all participants
- **Accountability**: Clear responsibilities and consequences
- **Adaptability**: Mechanisms to evolve the rules as needed
